Frequently Asked Questions

How does EndVision AI break down my goals?
Our AI system analyzes your goal description and applies principles from productivity methodologies to create a structured hierarchy of nodes, habits, and actions. You can always modify the AI suggestions to better fit your specific needs.
Can I transfer my data between devices?
Yes, your EndVision AI data is automatically synced across all devices where you're signed in with the same account. This includes goals, habits, actions, and time tracking data.
How do I track my progress towards goals?
EndVision AI provides several ways to track progress: visual progress bars for each goal, time tracking for habits and actions, completion percentages for nodes, and comprehensive analytics in the Insights dashboard.
Can I export my data from EndVision AI?
Yes, you can export your data from the Settings menu. This allows you to backup your information or transfer it to other systems if needed.
How do I create time templates?
Navigate to the Time Manager section and tap "Create Template." You can then select habits, actions, and tags that you frequently track together, and save them as a named template for easy access.
